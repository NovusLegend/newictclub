About Our ICT Club

Our Mission

The St. Joseph's Naggalama ICT Club is dedicated to fostering innovation, creativity, and technical skills among students. We believe in the power of technology to transform lives and communities.

Our motto "Lights, Code, Action" represents our approach: Illuminate ideas with knowledge (Lights), develop solutions through programming (Code), and implement real-world projects (Action).

Lights

Ignite your creativity and problem-solving skills through brainstorming sessions and tech workshops.

Code

Develop practical coding skills through hands-on projects in web development, mobile apps, and more.

Action

Implement your ideas and see them come to life while making a real impact in our community.
